Experiments were based on VCTK and LibriTTS dataset. All test samples have not appeared in the training set and validation set.

A2: added experiment for review: Robustness test of CampNet on the mask area's duration at the inference stage (insertion operation).

Note: In this experiment, we set different duration for the words to be inserted, and the duration range is large (the maximum duration can be close to three times the minimum duration). Through the experiment, we can find that the model has good robustness to the duration, and can automatically adjust the speed of the words to be edited with the change of duration.
